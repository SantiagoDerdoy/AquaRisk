Why AquaRisk?

AquaRisk converts groundwater uncertainty into measurable financial exposure. Our proprietary risk engine quantifies the probability and magnitude of capital impairment driven by hydrological stress.

Probability of Operational Impairment

Likelihood that groundwater levels fall below critical thresholds within a defined time horizon.

Monte Carlo simulation-based probability modeling.

Water Value at Risk (W-VaR)

Maximum expected financial loss associated with groundwater stress under adverse scenarios.

Confidence-interval based downside exposure metric.

Capital Exposure Index (CEI)

Quantifies total capital at risk by combining probability of threshold breach with asset valuation.

Proprietary groundwater risk indicator.
